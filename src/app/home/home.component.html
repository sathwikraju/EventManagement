Welcome to the Event Management App



Overview

The Event Management App is a powerful tool designed to streamline your organization's administrative tasks related to employee management. This application allows administrators to efficiently view, add, update, and delete employee details, all within a user-friendly interface.



How It Works


  1. Login: Upon successful login, administrators gain access to the dashboard.
  2. Dashboard: The dashboard provides quick links to different sections of the app. You can navigate to the "Home" page for an overview, the "Employees" page to manage employee data, and more.
  3. View Employees: In the "Employees" section, administrators can view a comprehensive list of employees. This list provides essential details for each employee, including their ID, first name, last name, and email.
  4. Add Employee: Easily add new employees to the system. Just provide the employee's first name, last name, and email, and the app will save the details for future reference.
  5. Employee Details: Clicking on an employee's record takes you to their detailed profile. Here, you can find additional information and get a closer look at individual employee data.
  6. Update and Delete: Administrators can update employee information whenever changes occur. Additionally, you have the option to remove an employee's record if they are no longer part of the organization.
